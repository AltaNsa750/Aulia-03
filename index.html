About Him

His Nickname

Carlos Sainz's nicknames are "Chilli" and "Smooth Operator." Chilli was given for his aggressive racing style and tenacious fighting spirit when he started his Formula 1 career. Smooth Operator was given to him for his calm and intelligent demeanor both on and off the track, as well as his excellent driving skills, inspired by Sade's song "Smooth Operator."

He Is On William's Teams

Carlos Sainz is a driver for Atlassian Williams Racing, racing alongside Alex Albon for the 2025 season and beyond. He joins the team after four years at Scuderia Ferrari, where he finished on the podium several times and achieved four race wins.

Where he lives

Carlos Sainz has been living in Monaco since 2024, just five minutes from fellow F1 driver Charles Leclerc. This decision was likely influenced by the advantages of being in a tax-free country like Monaco, which attracts many other F1 drivers.

Portfolio

Williams Company

Williams Racing is a British Formula 1 team founded by Sir Frank Williams and Patrick Head in 1977. The team is one of the most successful in Formula 1 history, winning nine Constructors' World Championship titles between 1980 and 1997. Renowned for its long history and strategic acumen, the team has seen a resurgence under new leadership that has brought improved performance and stability, highlighted by the arrival of Carlos Sainz as a driver for the 2025 season.
